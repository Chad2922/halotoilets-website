Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective date: April 2026

Overview

Halo Toilets ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Halo Toilets mobile application.

By using Halo Toilets, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Location Data — We access your device's GPS location to show nearby washrooms. This data is used only within the app and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties.
  • App Usage Data — We collect anonymous usage data to improve the app experience.
  • Crash Reports — If the app crashes, we collect diagnostic data to help us identify and fix issues.
  • Reports You Submit — If you submit a problem report about a location, we collect the report content and location reference to improve our data.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifying information
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not track your location history or store your location on our servers
  • We do not collect contacts, photos, or any other device data
  • We do not serve ads or share data with advertisers

How We Use Your Information

  • To show washrooms near your current location
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To process location problem reports you submit
  • To improve the accuracy of our washroom database

Third-Party Services

Halo Toilets uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps — For map display and directions. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase (Google) — For our database and crash reporting. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenStreetMap — As a source for washroom location data.

Data Retention

We do not store your personal location data. Problem reports you submit are retained to improve our database. Anonymous usage analytics are retained for up to 12 months.

Children's Privacy

Halo Toilets is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect personal information from any users, this app is safe for use by children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
